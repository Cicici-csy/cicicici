About Me

Hello! My name is Siyan Chen, and you can call me Cici. I was born in Sichuan, China, and I’m currently studying Interactive Media Arts at NYU Tisch School of the Arts.

My passions include design, interactive art, and physical computing. I enjoy hands-on experimentation and turning abstract ideas into tangible experiences through art and technology.

I’m also interested in web design and front-end development. Coding feels like solving creative puzzles, and I’m excited to keep improving my skills and building my own website.

I’m so happy to have you here—nice to meet you! Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect, collaborate, or just say hello. I’d love to hear from you.
